Welcome to my digital garden. 

I have been `journaling` since school. Recently I found one of my old journals, it was from class 7. Getting inspired from it, I decided to start writing once more. I could not write a proper sentence for an hour, only half sentences waiting to be completed by someone. 

Over the last year I have noticed that Artificial Intelligence has slowly killed my ability to write stuff without its aid. There have been situations where I just write some gibberish and then ask `ChatGPT` to restructure or reframe it. I have out sourced my thinking to the point that I find myself going to `LLMs` even when I have to take decisions (they might be important), send messages to groups (so that they look refined and perfect) or post on social media. I do not even bother to correct spelling mistakes (even when I know they are there), because of course `AI` would do it for me. 

I do not want to be so intellectually disabled that a day without `AI` would make me miserable. This `digital garden` will have my unfiltered words and thoughts all typed by me (even while typing this the urge to use `GPT` is high).

Here you will find : 
- `project logs` - They will contain the research, the decisions and why I took took them, how a certain piece of code works and why it works, notes and some other stuff. 

- `notes` - Will have explanations of things I am learning. This will be helpful for me to refer when I am stuck. 

- `TIL` - For quick stuff I learned or discovered during the day. 

- `ideas` - Will have half-baked ideas, hypothesis, things I want to explore. 

- `hobbies` - List of current and potential hobbies and how I am pursuing them. 

This is what I have planned to show here. You might find some other stuff also.
